💡 Visitor tips
🚆 Metra Electric: Take the Metra Electric to 111th St/Pullman — steps from the Visitor Center and Pullman Club Coffee. Trains run every 30–40 min from Millennium Park (~20 min express).
🚌 Getting here by CTA bus: Route #4 (Cottage Grove) runs the full length of the trail with stops at every block from 103rd to 115th St, connecting to the Red Line at 95th St. Routes #115 and #111A (Pullman Shuttle) also serve 111th–115th & Cottage Grove. Use the CTA Bus Tracker ↗ for real-time arrivals, and the Ventra app ↗ to pay for CTA and Metra rides in one place.
🚲 Divvy Bikes: Three docking stations near the trail — at Cottage Grove & 111th Pl, MLK Dr & 111th St, and near the 115th St Metra station. Use the Divvy or Lyft app to unlock bikes. divvybikes.com ↗
🅿️ Parking: Free parking available at the Exhibit Hall lot at 112th & Cottage Grove.
👟 Wear comfortable shoes — the full trail involves several blocks of walking on uneven historic brick sidewalks.
📅 Best days to visit: Thursday–Sunday when all four museums are open simultaneously.
🎟 Book Pullman House Project tours in advance — daily tours at 10am fill up, especially on weekends. Tickets at pullmanathome.org/tours.
☀️ Summer & fall are the best seasons — Arcade Park is lush, and the Annual House Tour happens each October.
